LaVeyan Satanism


I follow LaVeyan Satanism as my personal philosophy. Arguably the biggest misconception about Satanism is that Satanists worship the devil. This is simply untrue. Instead, Satanists like myself use Satan as a symbol of sovereignty, rational self-interest, and contempt for herd conformity. Not to be mistaken for The Satanic Temple, LaVeyan Satanism was the first religion to explicitly organize itself under the name "Satanism". Though heavily influenced by Ayn Rand, LaVeyan Satanism took egoism and anti-altruism, then fused them with ritual, symbolism, carnality, elitism, psychodrama, and the adversarial archetype of Satan.
